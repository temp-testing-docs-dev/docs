Tools allow you to extend the capabilities of your Vapi assistants by enabling them to perform specific actions or retrieve information from external sources. This functionality empowers your AI agents to handle more complex tasks and integrate with various services seamlessly.

## Available Tools

Vapi offers a range of built-in and custom tools to enhance your assistant's functionality:

- **Default Tools**: These include essential actions like transferring calls, ending calls, and dialing the keypad.
- **Custom Tools**: Create your own tools to integrate with external APIs or services.
- **Integration Tools**: Pre-built tools for popular services like Google Calendar, Google Sheets, and Slack.

## Integration Tools

- **Google Calendar**: Schedule appointments and check availability.
- **Google Sheets**: Read from and write to Google Sheets.
- **Slack**: Send messages to Slack channels or users.
- **Model Context Protocol (MCP)**: Connect to dynamic tools through MCP servers for enhanced capabilities.

## Key Features

- **Flexible Integration**: Easily connect your assistants to various external services and APIs.
- **Enhanced Functionality**: Enable your AI agents to perform complex tasks beyond simple conversation.
- **Customization**: Create tools tailored to your specific use case or industry needs.
- **Improved User Experience**: Provide more efficient and comprehensive assistance to your users.
